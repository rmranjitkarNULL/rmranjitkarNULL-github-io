About Me

I’m an embedded systems engineer studying ECE/RBE at WPI with a passion for building things from the ground up. I love designing and bringing up PCBs, programming MCUs, and exploring the possibilities of FPGAs. My goal is to work in the robotics field with a focus on embedded systems, while also continuing to grow my interests in computer vision, biomedical devices, and chip design.

Outside of work and academics, I like to stay active and creative in different ways. In the gym, I enjoy lifting weights and practicing karate as both a workout and a discipline. I also love cooking and experimenting with food from around the world—whether it’s making momos, a plate of katsu curry, or a fresh batch of snickerdoodles. When it’s time to relax, I boot up my PC, switching between favorites like Pokémon, Ultrakill, and 2XKO depending on what I’m into that week.
